About Me

My name is Angela Altamirano and I am from Sacramento, California. I am a junior at Cornell University studying Biomedical Engineering with a concentration in Biomechanics and Mechanobiology.

I am interested in biomedical engineering with the hope of creating a biomedical device that will allow for innovative healthcare options that are more accessible and affordable. Post-grad, I hope to work in R&D of medical devices to work towards making this a reality.

If I had to describe myself, I would say I am a storykeeper, not a storyteller. One reason why I chose to pursue BME is because of the people it serves. I love listening to the stories of others, especially about their experiences within the medical field and the ways it is not only good but can grow.

Outside of engineering, I enjoy learning my family's native languages: Tagalog and Spanish. Hopefully, in the future, I will be able to visit the Philippines and Mexico and communicate more effectively.
