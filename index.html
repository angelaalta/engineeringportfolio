Hi — I’m Angela. I design mechanical medical devices and prototypes.

I focus on mechanical design, rapid prototyping, and validation testing. Recent projects include a self-stabilizing cane, a flexible fastener alternative to buttons, and a device for lung fluid monitoring. I enjoy moving concepts from sketch → CAD → prototype → test.
